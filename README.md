# 🚀 Autonomous Enterprise Digital Twin Platform (ServiceNow)

## 📌 Overview
An AI-powered Digital Twin platform built on ServiceNow to simulate, monitor, and optimize enterprise operations in real time.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Enterprises struggle with:
- Reactive incident management
- Lack of real-time operational visibility
- No predictive intelligence

---

## 💡 Solution
This platform creates a Digital Twin of enterprise operations and provides:
- Real-time state modeling
- Dynamic risk scoring
- AI-driven insights and recommendations

---

## 🏗️ Architecture
- ServiceNow Custom Table (Digital Twin)
- Business Rules (Event-driven automation)
- AI Logic Layer (Scoring Engine)
- Dashboard (Executive Insights)

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

### 🔹 Digital Twin Modeling
- Multi-domain (IT, HR, Customer)

### 🔹 Event-Driven Automation
- Incident → Twin auto-creation
- Real-time updates

### 🔹 AI Intelligence Engine
- Dynamic risk scoring
- Health and anomaly detection

### 🔹 Executive Dashboard
- Risk distribution
- Health insights
- Status breakdown

---

## 🧪 Demo Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Critical Incident
- Priority = High
- Result: High risk, low health, escalation recommendation

### Scenario 2: Resolved Incident
- Result: Health improves, risk reduces
